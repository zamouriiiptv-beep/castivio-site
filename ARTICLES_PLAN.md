## قائمة النشر (checklist لكل مقال)

1. لصق النص في محرر Medium (السطر الأول = العنوان)
2. تحويل الروابط: ظلّل الرابط النصي كاملاً ← الصق فوقه (يتحول لرابط فعّال تلقائياً)
3. العناوين الفرعية: ظلّل السطر ← Bold
4. صورة غلاف مقصوصة (بدون شريط حالة/متصفح) من الصفحة المستهدفة
5. الوسوم (5): Streaming · IPTV · Cord Cutting · Technology · Entertainment (أو Television)
6. Unlist story = مطفأ ← Publish
7. بعد النشر: ربط عبارة "my previous review" بمقال سابق من السلسلة (شبكة داخلية)
8. طلب فهرسة الصفحات المستهدفة في Google Search Console
9. مشاركة رابط المقال في حساب تواصل واحد على الأقل

## نص المقال 2 (جاهز)

```
Cutting the Cord in the US, UK, and Canada: My Honest Castivio Experience

Cable bills keep climbing on both sides of the Atlantic, and more households are looking for a simpler way to watch live content. After covering Castivio's European editions in my previous review, several readers asked how the platform performs in English-speaking countries. So I spent a few weeks testing it the way a regular household would — evening news, weekend sports, and kids' programming. Here's what I found.

Why cord-cutters are switching

Traditional cable locks you into long contracts, bulky equipment, and channel bundles you never watch. A streaming subscription flips that model: you pay a fraction of the price, install an app on the devices you already own, and watch in HD or 4K wherever you are. Castivio takes this further with 8K support on premium plans and Anti-Freeze technology that keeps live streams smooth during peak hours.

Castivio in the United States

The US edition focuses on what American households actually watch: national news, live sports, and family entertainment, with full lineups in crisp 4K. Setup on Firestick and Roku took me under ten minutes. You can read the full breakdown here: https://castivio.com/guide/usa/

Castivio in the United Kingdom

British viewers get the national favourites — news, sport, and award-winning drama — with dependable evening performance, which is exactly when most UK services buckle. The UK guide covers everything from Smart TV setup to broadband requirements: https://castivio.com/guide/uk/

Castivio in Canada

Canada is where the platform surprised me most: English and French content under one subscription, national news, and coverage that works from Toronto to Vancouver. Details and setup steps are here: https://castivio.com/guide/canada/

The verdict

Across all three countries, the experience was consistent: fast activation, stable 4K streams, responsive WhatsApp support, and yearly plans from just €30. If you're paying for cable in 2026, you owe it to your wallet to compare. Start with the monthly plan and see the difference for yourself.
```
