About Us

Get to know Castivio

Castivio is a digital broadcasting service built to make entertainment simple, reliable and accessible for households everywhere. Our mission is to offer families a straightforward way to enjoy the content they love, on the devices they already own, without the complexity of traditional cable.

Who we are

Castivio is operated by Mohammed Bouinidane, Founder & Managing Director, based in Rabat-Salé, Morocco. We are an independent, owner-operated service focused on quality of experience rather than volume.

Our mission

We believe entertainment should be effortless. Our goal is to deliver a stable, high-definition viewing experience with fair pricing and responsive human support — not automated chatbots.

Our values

  • Transparency — clear pricing, clear plans, no hidden terms.
  • Reliability — engineering that prioritises stable playback during peak hours.
  • Respect — for our customers, their time and their data.
  • Legality — full compliance with international copyright and takedown frameworks.

How to reach us

Email us at support@castivio.com or reach our team on WhatsApp . We reply within 24 hours.
