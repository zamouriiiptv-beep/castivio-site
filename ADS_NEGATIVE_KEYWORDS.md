## Why these negatives exist

The UK Search campaign received clicks from searches unrelated to our service
(competitor brand names, "free" intent, piracy app names, generic "how to watch"
research queries). These drain budget with **0 conversions**. Each negative below
was chosen from real Search Terms report data or as pre-emptive protection.

Match-type notes:
- **Broad negative** (no quotes) — blocks the word in any query.
- **Phrase negative** ("...") — blocks the exact phrase in that order.

## Workflow

1. Pull the **Search Terms report** (last 5–7 days).
2. Flag terms with clicks + 0 conversions or clearly off-topic intent.
3. Cross-check against the ADDED tables above — skip anything already listed.
4. Add the new negatives in Google Ads (campaign-level negative list).
5. Move them from CANDIDATES → a new "ADDED — Batch N" table here, with date.
